How We Build

Transform for Equity is a consultancy that helps organizations achieve racial equity. They do this by providing customized facilitation and coaching support for systemic racial equity transformation. They have over 25 years of experience working with organizations in the public and private sector across the country. Their work is grounded in the belief that racial equity is essential for a just and thriving society.

Collaboration

Identify levels or understanding and create a safe space to come together. Establish clear goals and foster dialogue about team dynamics.

Workshops

Tailored and targeted workshops to increase institutional knowledge and spur change.

Capacity Building

Facilitation coaching to scale the antiracist work to your organization.

Discussions

Intensive facilitated small group discussions to raise self-awareness of race, the impact of race in our society and communities.

Organizational Assessment

Organizational assessments to help understand where structural inequities exist and provide strategies to improve diversity, equity and belonging within the institution.

Retrospectives

Individual coaching for organizational leaders to support centering antiracism thinking and action.
